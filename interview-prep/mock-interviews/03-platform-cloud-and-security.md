# Mock Interview 3: Platform, Cloud, CI/CD, And Security

Use this to practice platform-engineering breadth with senior tradeoffs.

## Questions

1. Design a secure internal platform on GCP for services running mainly on GKE.
2. What parts of that design would change in AWS, and what should stay conceptually the same?
3. How do you know what is running in production came from reviewed source?
4. How would you design a multi-tenant CI platform with different trust levels?
5. What are the most important default guardrails for product teams?
6. How would you implement break-glass access without undermining normal safety controls?
7. A policy is technically correct but teams constantly bypass it. What do you do?
8. How do you balance platform standardization with cloud-native advantages?
9. What signals tell you your delivery platform itself is becoming a reliability risk?
10. Describe a platform incident that starts as a security concern and turns into a reliability incident.

## Interviewer Follow-Ups

- where is the real blast radius
- what would you centralize
- what would you leave to teams
- what is the rollback story
- what does auditability mean in practice

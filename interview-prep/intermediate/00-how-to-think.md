# Intermediate: How To Think Under Ambiguity

At this level, the interviewer wants to see that you can narrow a messy production problem without panicking.

## Golden Pattern

1. Confirm blast radius.
2. Confirm whether the issue is steady, intermittent, or load-related.
3. Locate the first broken layer.
4. Compare healthy versus unhealthy paths.
5. Prioritize reversible actions first.

## Mentor Tips

- compare one good node and one bad node whenever possible
- explain why you chose the next command
- say what evidence would make you change direction
- distinguish symptom, signal, and cause

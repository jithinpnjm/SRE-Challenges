# Kubernetes Lab 5: Operators, Mesh, Policy, And Disaster Recovery Review

## Scenario

A platform team runs many operators, uses admission policies, is considering service mesh adoption, and claims the cluster is DR-ready.

## Your Task

Write and sketch:

1. operator risk model
2. policy and admission strategy
3. service mesh decision framework
4. etcd and cluster recovery approach
5. stateful workload recovery plan
6. what "DR-ready" would actually require

## Model-Answer Rubric

- does the answer distinguish controller risk from workload risk
- does it explain when policy helps and when it becomes incident fragility
- does it describe mesh value and overhead honestly
- does it separate etcd, cluster, and application recovery clearly
- does it avoid hand-wavy "multi-cluster solves DR" logic

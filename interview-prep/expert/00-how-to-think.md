# Expert: How To Think Like A Senior Production Owner

Senior interview performance is not just technical depth. It is also calm prioritization, tradeoff clarity, and operational leadership.

## Golden Pattern

1. Clarify the business and technical stakes.
2. Name likely failure domains.
3. Separate detection, diagnosis, mitigation, and prevention.
4. Favor blast-radius reduction before elegance.
5. State tradeoffs explicitly.

## Mentor Tips

- do not sound surprised by failure
- design for operator reality, not only ideal architecture
- mention rollback, observability, and dependency behavior early
- if asked for a fix, include validation and failure containment
